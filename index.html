The Four C's

Our expertise is rooted in the fundamentals of diamond quality, providing you with transparent grading for every stone.

  • Cut: The primary factor in a diamond's sparkle. A precise cut unleashes its brilliant fire.
  • Color: Graded on a scale from D (colorless) to Z, true purity is found in the absence of color.
  • Clarity: A measure of a diamond's natural imperfections. We select stones of exceptional clarity.
  • Carat: The measure of a diamond's weight, not necessarily its size.
